New Arrangement Category Update, Thursday 1st of April 2021

April 01, 2021, 03:34:47 PM Posted by: Libera
Greetings everyone!  We have an extreme-ly important update today:

By far the number one complaint we get at NinSheetMusic is that our arrangements are just simply too easy to play and don't challenge our users enough.  The updating team has been thinking about this problem for a long time now and we are excited to finally announce our solution: Extreme Version arrangements!  For too long our sheets have been constrained by authenticity, standard practice, and by trying to stick to an appropriate difficulty for the original piece.  This new arrangement category will allow us to explore bold new ways of arranging, never before considered possible.

Unfortunately, talented as they are, our current arrangers' skills would nevertheless be insufficient to introduce this brave new category, so we have had to bring in a team of legendary, brand new, never before seen arrangers to put together this exciting launch update.  We hope that you are all as excited as we are about this whole new extreme realm of possiblities that are opened to us now.
